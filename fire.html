Fire-Rated Rolling Shutters

Fire-rated shutters are specially designed and certified to resist fire for 2–4 hours. They automatically close during emergencies to prevent the spread of flames and smoke, making them essential for factories, kitchens, and high-risk facilities.

  • Certified fire resistance for 2–4 hours
  • Automatic closing mechanism during emergencies
  • Prevents spread of fire and smoke
  • Ideal for factories, commercial kitchens, and high-security facilities
